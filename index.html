2 Way Conversation

Chat with anyone using SMS from your desktop to their cell phone. Instantly start chatting with your clients, staff and future customers.

Live Support

Assign an agent(s) as live support so you'll have someone there to help a new customer when they are inquiring about your business or company.

Staff and Departments

Communicate with your staff through C8. Create chat groups by department to keep things organized and assign conversations to the correct staff member or department when a new client reaches out to your company.

Outbound calling

If you are talking with a customer/subscriber and need to talk to them directly, simply call them. With transcripts and recording you will be able to see and hear how your staff is conversating with your customers.

Reputation Builder

Once you've had a positive interaction with a client, give them a reputation feedback link that will help your Google ratings. Once people start to see how helpful your services are, your business will begin to grow!

Customer and Subscriber Profile

Get to know your customers and subscribers. Begin detailing your customers profiles. Putting your clients in the correct list will help you get a better idea on what target audience you are looking for.

Broadcasting messages

Send your message to your targeted audience within seconds, or set up a preferred time for your message(s) to be sent out. Being engaging with new customers every day!

Reports

Send your detailed reports to your email, get notified daily on reports to see how things went for the day. Download reports to analyze how the business is performing. From Chat Reports, Metrics, Customer Reports, Usage History and more.

Full Feature Breakdown

  • Text Broadcasting

    Dramatically boost your brand awareness and marketing campaigns by sending bulk messages to contact groups, while being able to track the statistics of recurring messages.

  • Email Broadcasting

    Having bulk email capabilities improves credibility, brand awareness, and sales.

  • Click2Call Outbound Calling

    Make calls directly from within the app to secure leads when they’re actively engaged.

  • Click2Call Transcription & Recording

    Improve your customer service by being able to review previous phone calls for quality assurance and training purposes, or take advantage of pre-recorded messages for outbound calling to gain more time and revenue.

  • Call Blocking

    Cut back on delays, wasted time, and add more overall value to your service by being able to block unwanted calls.

  • Text Autoresponders

    Keep leads engaged with Convers8ions pre-enabled auto messaging capabilities.

  • Email Autoresponders

    Enable sequence emails to continue to keep warm leads engaged and secure them straight away.

  • FB Audience Builder

    Use one of the largest social media platforms to your advantage while our software automatically retargets your audience based on your current contacts, and helps to close even more sales.

  • Coupon Builder

    Design custom promotions or coupons, and generate more business by easily distributing them through email or SMS messaging all from one database.

  • Round Robin

    Allow team members to ‘phone a friend’ and rotate conversations to have more than one team member troubleshooting or addressing a client’s needs at a time.

  • Appointments

    Capture leads more efficiently by scheduling right within the platform.

  • Webchat Website Widget

    Create a chat widget to embed on any website and allow leads to instantly create email, text, or messenger conversations with your team members at the click of a button.

  • LeadProtect

    Maintain confidentiality by shielding customer profiles to protect their personal information and secure their privacy, but also the privacy of your team and operations with advanced security settings.

  • Departments

    Days of tracking down the right person are over - be able to assign various operations and conversations to pre-designated departments to make communications and work-flow easier.

  • Keyword Auto-Replies

    Provide instant interaction by enabling automated responses based on keywords or phrases, and maintaining client engagement.

  • Conversation Sharing

    Connect clients with specialist team members right away, by being able to transfer leads and communications with ease for both you and your customers.

  • Conversation Tagging

    Create automated settings to group leads and customers based on specific criteria, and alert other team members of higher priority conversations.

  • Review/Referral Generator

    Enable automatic email and SMS follow up messages to generate more reviews and referrals from your customers.
